Introduction
This page consists of reporting the situation that is being living in Mexico and the actions that we should put in practice to become a developed country.

It is very important that people realize all the situations that happen in our country, since sometimes we do not realize the changes and surpasses that there are.

Thanks to this information that is presented, we need that people become aware of the situation in which we are living and that we put into practice our knowledge and skills to be able to convert our country in a better place.

The actions that we must follow to transform our country in a better developed:



1) Reduce taxes on gasoline and diesel for avoid increases in public transport and products basic

2) Build new refineries, because the ones we have are obsolete If we produce our own gasoline, it will be cheaper.

3) Make public oil revenues to know how much is produced and how this money is spent.

4) Betting on the use of railways to move merchandise and passengers. This will produce costs of transport and will promote tourism.

5) Implement a strategy to avoid the exit of investments of the country. If Donald Trump presses companies not to establish themselves in Mexico, we must show that we have the talent so that even invest more here.

6) Reduce the salary of the president, governors secretaries of state, deputies, senators, and seniors officials, in addition to the pensions of former Presidents The saved would not represent much, but it is not possible that we have officials who earn in a month, more than a house of social interest that Mexicans are slow to pay more than 20 years

7) Eliminate advertising expenses, events and ceremonies government, as well as land and air transport of officials. Also, cut the allocation of resources for political parties and their campaigns.

8) Effectively fight corruption and impunity. we must enforce the law to confront and punish the corrupt, regardless of who they are or the since it has.

9) Invest well in education to have a society more and better educated that requires better governments and Strengthen our democracy. Education is the best weapon to fight the problems of Mexico.
